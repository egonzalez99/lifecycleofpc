The Creation of Windows OS

The Microsoft operating system was developed in 1982 after Bill Gates was impressed and influenced by the Visi On at COMDEX in 1982. After the visit Bill Gates approved of the concept of developing an operating system with a GUI [Improvement over MUSH]. Microsoft combined two teams called GDI [graphics device interface] and Interface Manager into the Microsoft Window Manager team. It was developed using Assembly and C languages.

Cloud Computing

Microsoft is a major player in the cloud computing market by offering Azure. Cloud computing was seen as a niche market and infrastructure to invest like 10+ years. However, Microsoft saw an advantage in the market by becoming an early investor. Azure has been an essential tool in databases, analytics, networking, and artifical intelligence.
