The Creation of Windows OS

The Microsoft operating system was developed in 1982 after Bill Gates was impressed and influenced by the Visi On at COMDEX in 1982. After the visit Bill Gates approved of the concept of developing an operating system with a GUI [Improvement over MUSH]. Microsoft combined two teams called GDI [graphics device interface] and Interface Manager into the Microsoft Window Manager team. It was developed using Assembly and C languages. While Window 1.0 was being developed Apple and Microsoft had conflicts when it came to whether Windows stole the GUI from Apple during there partnership.
